Fire Breathing Magician

A bit of danger is exciting — when it’s controlled. Moswatii the Magician brings you a fire performance that’s bold, cinematic and carefully managed from the first spark to the final bow.

Fire is not just a stunt; it’s discipline. I’ve trained to understand flame behavior, fuels and timing so your audience can enjoy the thrill without worry. Every appearance is planned with the venue and production team to fit the space and your show flow.

The best fire magic happens outdoors or in large, well-ventilated spaces with a clear performance zone. I’ve performed under tough conditions — wind, tight run-sheets, unique staging — and those challenges built the calm, prepared approach I bring to your event.

Safety, always

  • • Pre-event risk assessment and brief with your team
  • • Fire blankets/extinguishers on hand & clear guest distance
  • • Non-flammable costuming & controlled fuel handling
  • • Venue approval required; outdoor/ventilated areas preferred

Certain venues/municipalities may require permits or additional insurance — I’ll advise during booking.
